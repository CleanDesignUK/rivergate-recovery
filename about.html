About Rivergate Recovery

Discover my single-person, online approach to addiction recovery and how I serve individuals throughout North Wales.

My Mission & Values

I believe everyone deserves a supportive, personalised environment to overcome addiction. Through one-to-one online sessions, I combine evidence-based therapies, compassion, and a deep respect for your unique circumstances. Serving Wrexham, Colwyn Bay, Rhyl, Bangor, and beyond, my mission is to guide each client toward lasting change without the need for an inpatient facility or rotating staff.

  • Respect & Individuality
  • Evidence-Based & Holistic Methods
  • Accessible Online Support

My Background & Approach

With a background in clinical psychology and specialised training in addiction recovery, I’ve worked with clients facing alcohol, drug, and behavioural dependencies. Over the years, I noticed many people prefer the confidentiality and convenience of online therapy. That’s why I focus on a single-person practice—ensuring continuity, trust, and a direct line of communication between you and me.

From exploring emotional triggers to designing relapse prevention strategies, I tailor every session to align with your goals. Whether you’re a high functioning alcoholic seeking discreet help or someone coping with substance misuse for the first time, my online format provides an approachable way to start or continue your recovery.
