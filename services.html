My Comprehensive Services

One-to-one addiction support to help you find lasting recovery and prevent relapse. Whether you need guidance to help me stop drinking alcohol or to address drug abuse treatment concerns, I offer a single-person approach tailored to you.

Personalised Addiction Support

My services aim to meet you where you are—whether you consider yourself a high functioning alcoholic, struggle with painkiller addiction, or simply need discreet alcohol support. Through direct online sessions, we’ll explore your unique needs and craft a realistic plan to manage triggers and maintain your progress.

I recognise that every individual’s journey is different. Some want to break harmful patterns quickly, while others need more time. By focusing on your personal history, motivations, and goals, I provide a supportive framework that fosters long-term recovery—no inpatient facility or rotating staff involved.

Core Services & Approaches

12-Step Programme

A classic, structured path encouraging honesty, community, and accountability. Suitable for both alcohol-related and other misuse challenges.

CBT (Cognitive Behavioural Therapy)

Therapy Practices & Techniques

Depending on your circumstances—be it help me stop drinking alcohol or learning coping strategies for drug abuse treatment—I blend various evidence-based methods, such as:

  • Motivational Interviewing (MI) to boost your commitment to change
  • Mindfulness & Relaxation for craving management
  • Relapse Prevention Planning for long-term stability
  • Family Systems Approach for healthier relationships
  • Brief Solution-Focused Interventions for immediate problem-solving
  • Trauma-Informed Care for a safe, supportive environment

I adapt these techniques to your evolving needs, ensuring you have the right tools to address triggers and foster lasting recovery. With a single-person, online model, you’ll experience consistent care in a private setting.
