Our Comprehensive Services

We offer a wide range of programmes tailored to support individuals on their journey to sobriety and wellness. Explore our detox options, 12-step guidance, inpatient/outpatient care, and aftercare solutions.

Detoxification

Safe and medically supervised detox to manage withdrawal symptoms for alcohol, opioids, and other substances.

  • 24/7 Clinical Care
  • Medication-Assisted
Inpatient & Outpatient Care

Flexible treatment options that meet you where you are. Benefit from structured inpatient programmes or maintain daily responsibilities with outpatient support.

  • Residential Rehab
  • Day Programmes
Therapy & Counselling

Evidence-based approaches including CBT, DBT, group therapy, and family sessions to address underlying triggers.

  • One-on-One Sessions
  • Family Workshops
12-Step Programme

A classic framework for long-term sobriety, including peer support and spiritual growth.

  • Fellowship Meetings
  • Community Network
Aftercare & Relapse Prevention

Ongoing support post-treatment, including sober coaching, alumni groups, and relapse-prevention planning.

  • Sober Coaching
  • Alumni Network
Family & Community Engagement

Workshops and group sessions to heal relationships, build understanding, and nurture lasting support networks.

  • Family Therapy
  • Local Resource Guides
