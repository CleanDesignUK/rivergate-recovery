Understanding Compulsive Gambling

I specialise in helping individuals who struggle with gambling addiction—whether it’s high-stakes betting, online casinos, or frequent lottery plays. Many people in Wrexham, Colwyn Bay, Rhyl, and Bangor find themselves caught in a cycle of rising debt, relationship difficulties, and emotional distress.

Gambling addiction doesn’t always present itself outwardly—some may appear to be functioning gamblers with stable jobs and social lives. In reality, the pressure to chase losses and the secrecy involved can lead to anxiety, depression, or strained family dynamics.

Through online, one-to-one sessions, I help uncover the root causes of compulsive gambling and set a path to meaningful change—without judgement or stigma.

Regain Control & Hope

My approach combines expertise in addiction counselling with a deep empathy for those facing gambling problems.
