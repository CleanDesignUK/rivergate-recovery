Understanding Drug Misuse in North Wales

From painkiller addiction to recreational stimulant abuse, drug misuse has devastating consequences for individuals and their families. In areas like Wrexham, Conwy, Rhyl, and Bangor, many struggle with hidden substance misuse, unsure where to turn for real solutions.

At Rivergate Recovery, we believe that every person deserves access to compassionate, effective drug abuse treatment. Whether you require an inpatient programme or prefer an outpatient approach, our diverse array of services can be tailored to your unique needs.

Our Comprehensive Approach

Medically Monitored Detox

Experience a secure environment for withdrawal management, supervised by our clinical experts. We address prescription drug dependence, opioid addiction, and more, ensuring you transition to sobriety safely.

Evidence-Based Therapies

Our programmes encompass Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), and other modalities, enabling you to break the destructive thought patterns linked to substance misuse.

Family & Peer Support

Overcoming addiction is easier when you have a reliable support network. We encourage family counselling, community group sessions, and ongoing peer engagement to sustain your recovery.

Bespoke Plans Led by Field Experts

No two individuals experience substance misuse in the same way. That’s why we craft custom treatment paths, drawing on the expertise of addiction specialists, psychologists, and medical professionals. We guide you from detox to rehab, ensuring every phase addresses your specific situation.

Our track record has helped countless people in North Wales reclaim their lives. Whether it’s helping drug addicts who’ve tried multiple programmes or supporting those new to treatment, Rivergate Recovery stands at the forefront of compassionate, evidence-based care.

Long-Term Aftercare & Support

Achieving sobriety is just the beginning. To prevent relapse and strengthen your resilience, we provide continuing care solutions, sober coaching, and community outreach. Our mission is to keep you connected to meaningful recovery resources for the long term.
