Online Alcohol Help & Support

Whether you’re a high functioning alcoholic or struggling with alcohol dependence, get the help quitting alcohol you need. Manage alcohol cravings and explore detox for alcohol from the comfort of home.

Help Me Stop Drinking Alcohol

Understanding Alcohol Misuse Across North Wales

If you find it difficult to manage even 6 days with no alcohol, or you’re worried about your role as a functioning alcoholic, you’re not alone. I specialise in helping individuals in Wrexham, Colwyn Bay, Rhyl, and Bangor break free from alcohol dependence through secure, online sessions—no group facilities or 24-hour staff.

Many clients reach out when they hear themselves saying, “Help me stop drinking alcohol” or “I need help quitting alcohol.” My approach addresses root causes, such as stress, trauma, or social pressures, while equipping you with practical strategies to prevent relapse and manage alcohol cravings.

Let’s work together to transform your relationship with drinking and restore control, health, and confidence in your daily life.

My Online Alcohol Recovery Approach

Detox & Managed Withdrawal

Although I don’t provide inpatient services, I collaborate with healthcare providers to guide you through detox for alcohol safely. My role is to help you prepare for withdrawal, identify local medical resources, and develop an actionable plan for your initial steps toward sobriety.

  • Non-Judgemental Advice
  • Close Coordination with Your GP (if needed)
Therapy & Counselling

I integrate cognitive and behavioural techniques to address underlying issues fueling your alcohol misuse. Through one-to-one video calls, we’ll explore triggers, coping strategies, and personal goals, enabling you to develop a healthier mindset and maintain motivation in the face of alcohol cravings.

  • CBT & DBT Techniques
  • Personal Action Plans
Family & Loved Ones

Living with an alcoholic can strain even the strongest relationships. If desired, I can guide family members through effective communication, boundary setting, and supportive practices—so everyone moves forward together in a more positive environment.

  • Optional Family Sessions
  • Education & Resource Sharing

Bespoke Solutions & Aftercare

Every client’s journey looks different. Because I’m a sole practitioner, you’ll always see the same person each session—ensuring consistent, empathetic, and individually tailored care. There’s no large facility or revolving staff.

After the initial stages of recovery, I offer ongoing check-ins, sober coaching, and relapse-prevention planning. If you need local resources or community groups for additional alcohol support, I’ll connect you with the right contacts.

Let’s craft a sustainable path to a healthier, more fulfilling life, free from the grip of alcohol.
