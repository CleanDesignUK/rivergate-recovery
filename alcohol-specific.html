Understanding Alcohol Misuse in North Wales

Whether you consider yourself a functioning alcoholic or find it difficult to manage even 6 days with no alcohol, the impact of excessive drinking cannot be underestimated. From Wrexham to Colwyn Bay, and from Rhyl to Bangor, communities across North Wales are grappling with the serious consequences of alcohol dependence.

Rising costs of alcohol haven't decreased its availability or harm. If you’re living with an alcoholic, you already know the toll it can take on finances, relationships, and personal well-being. Our mission at Rivergate Recovery is to provide comprehensive, local support to those who find themselves saying, “I need help quitting alcohol” or “Help me stop drinking alcohol” before the damage becomes irreversible.

There is hope—our team is here to guide you through every stage of the recovery process.

Our Alcohol Recovery Approach

Medically Supervised Detox

Our detox for alcohol programme ensures a safe, clinically monitored process for individuals seeking to remove toxins from their body under expert guidance.

Therapeutic Programmes

From 12-step programmes to personalised alcohol therapy, our range of evidence-based methods addresses the root causes and fosters long-term recovery.

Family & Community Support

Living with an alcoholic affects everyone. We offer group counselling, family workshops, and local support with alcohol to rebuild trust and strengthen relationships.

Tailored Solutions Backed by Experts

Our team comprises certified counsellors, medical professionals, and support staff dedicated to ensuring each client receives a unique, individual plan. Whether you're seeking stop drinking assistance for the first time or you've experienced multiple relapses, we design flexible options including inpatient, outpatient, and community-based programmes across North Wales.

We believe in compassionate, non-judgemental care. Through structured counselling sessions, relapse prevention strategies, and peer support, our focus is on sustainable change—helping you reclaim control of your life.

Let us be the turning point in your journey to a healthier, alcohol-free life.

Aftercare & Continuous Support

Recovery doesn’t stop when initial treatment ends. Our dedicated aftercare programmes and sober coaching help maintain your momentum long after you leave our facility. If you or someone you care about needs help with alcohol near you, trust Rivergate Recovery’s network of resources to keep you on the right path.
