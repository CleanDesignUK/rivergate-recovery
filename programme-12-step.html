My Online 12-Step Programme

A flexible, one-to-one approach to overcoming addiction, rooted in the 12-step model of peer support, personal growth, and spiritual reflection—entirely online.

Core Steps & Principles

  1. Admitting powerlessness over addiction.
  2. Believing in a power greater than ourselves.
  3. Turning our will and lives over to that power.
  4. Conducting a fearless moral inventory.
  5. Admitting the exact nature of our wrongs to ourselves, another human being, and a higher power.

Each step emphasizes honesty, openness, and the willingness to change. My 12-step programme fosters personal insight, accountability, and spiritual growth (in any form you define), helping to reduce the risk of relapse and promote a fulfilling sober recovery.

How My Programme Works

I run this programme entirely online through one-to-one video sessions. This structure ensures privacy, convenience, and a tailored experience. Whether you’re new to recovery or searching for a clear, structured path, these steps will help you cultivate self-reflection, accountability, and renewed purpose.

  • Weekly Virtual Meetings
  • Individualised Mentorship
  • Integration with Other Therapies

My 12-step approach complements other substance misuse treatments you may be undertaking (e.g., for alcohol dependence or drug addiction). It blends personal responsibility with a supportive spiritual or communal framework to strengthen long-term commitment.

Serving Individuals Across North Wales

Although my 12-step sessions happen online, I serve clients throughout Wrexham, Colwyn Bay, Rhyl, Bangor, and beyond. The virtual format means you can access guidance without commuting, ensuring consistent attendance and progress.

These sessions invite you to explore moral inventories, relinquish destructive habits, and build a fresh foundation for lasting sobriety. I honour each client’s perspective, whether it’s religious, spiritual, or secular.

I invite you to join this supportive network and let the 12 steps guide you toward genuine, life-altering change.

Begin Your 12-Step Journey

If you’re ready to embrace the 12-step method in a private, online format, contact me today. Whether you need help with alcohol dependence, drug addiction, or another form of substance misuse, I’m here to support you each step of the way.
